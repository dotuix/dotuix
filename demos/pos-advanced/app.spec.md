1. **Login** — staff avatar selection + 4-digit PIN pad
2. **Catalog** — category tab strip, product grid with search, live cart sidebar
3. **Checkout** — cart review, discount input, payment method selection (cash/card/split)
4. **Orders** — recent transaction history with receipt modal
5. **Reports** — daily/weekly sales summary, top products, staff performance — gated via uix.license.hasFeature("reports")
6. **Settings** — data export (.uixdata bundle), import, staff management, system info
